About Us

At International Interactive Technologies (IIT), we help Shopify and subscription-based brands grow by turning customer data into smarter decisions. We believe technology should drive revenue and opportunity — not complexity.

We specialize in retention optimization, subscription analytics, and growth-focused Shopify management. Our work focuses on increasing customer lifetime value (LTV), improving repeat purchase rates, and uncovering hidden revenue opportunities within your existing customer base.

Our background spans eCommerce systems, data analytics, automation, and technical support — allowing us to not only identify growth opportunities, but also implement the systems that make them sustainable.

We partner with brands that want more than just traffic. They want better retention, smarter operations, and data-backed growth strategies.

Our Approach: We combine hands-on Shopify experience with analytics and automation expertise. That means we don’t just provide insights — we help put the right systems in place to support long-term growth.

Our Vision: To be a trusted growth partner for modern Shopify brands seeking smarter, more profitable operations.

Our Mission: Help eCommerce businesses increase repeat revenue through data, retention strategies, and reliable technical systems.
