About

Built by someone who works inside Shopify and subscription data every day.

International Interactive is run by Matt Ammirati, a Shopify developer with a background in data engineering. That combination means stores aren't just built to look good at launch — they're built with the customer and subscription data underneath in mind from day one.

Most of the work here centers on subscription commerce: Recharge and similar subscription apps, repeat-purchase flows, and the kind of store architecture that makes recurring revenue easier to see and easier to grow. Store audits, GraphQL/API integration, and point-of-sale work round out the rest.

For some clients that's a single project. For others it's ongoing technical ownership — being the one person who knows the store's codebase, its apps, and the reasoning behind past decisions, rather than whoever's available that week.
